## 1. Overview

Dari for Business is a stablecoin payment infrastructure platform designed to help businesses accept, manage, and automate payments using blockchain-based digital dollars such as USDT and USDC.

The platform provides tools similar to traditional payment processors but optimized for blockchain transactions.

**Key capabilities include:**
- Payment links
- Invoice generation
- Subscription billing
- APIs for developers
- Webhooks for automation
- Multi-chain stablecoin payments
- Instant settlements

The pricing model is designed as a tiered SaaS subscription combined with transaction fees, allowing businesses of different sizes to adopt the platform easily.

The model includes:
- **Free tier** for small users
- **Growth tier** for startups
- **Business tier** for scaling companies
- **Enterprise tier** for institutions

---

## 2. Pricing Structure

Dari for Business operates using two pricing components:

1. **Monthly subscription fee** – Access to platform features and limits
2. **Transaction fee** – Small percentage charged on every payment processed

## 10. Competitive Advantage

Dari for Business focuses on advantages that traditional payment processors cannot provide.

**Key differentiators include:**

### Instant Stablecoin Settlement
Businesses receive funds instantly without bank settlement delays (vs. 1-3 days with traditional processors).

### Cross-Border Payments
Global payments can be completed within minutes using stablecoins, eliminating slow international wire transfers.

### Multi-Chain Payment Routing
Transactions are automatically routed through the lowest-cost blockchain network, optimizing fees.

### Gasless Payments
End users do not need cryptocurrency or pay gas fees. All blockchain costs are handled by the merchant (charged in the transaction fee).

### No Bank Requirements
Businesses can accept payments without traditional banking infrastructure.

### Transparent Pricing
No hidden fees. All costs are transparent and predictable.

## 13. Onboarding by Tier

All merchants start with a simple 3-step onboarding:

1. **Signup** – Email/Password or Google OAuth
2. **Business Details** – Business name, category, country
3. **Wallet Setup** – Auto-generate wallets on selected blockchains

After onboarding:
- **Free Plan** merchants can immediately create payment links
- **Growth+** merchants get API access for custom integrations
- **Enterprise** merchants work with dedicated implementation team

## 17. Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change plans at any time. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept USDC, USDT, and other stablecoins for subscription payments. Invoice and ACH coming soon for Enterprise.

### Do you offer discounts for long-term commitments?
Yes. Annual plans get 15% discount. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom terms.

### What about refunds?
Payment processing is non-refundable. Subscription fees can be refunded within 14 days if you haven't used the service.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any tier. Start for free and upgrade when needed.

### Can I white-label the platform?
Yes, white-label is available on the Enterprise plan. Growth/Business plans can use add-ons for custom branding.

### What support do you offer?
- **Free/Growth:** Community support + Help center (24-48hr response)
- **Business:** Email support (24hr response)
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated account manager + phone support (1hr response)

### Do you have a free trial?
Start with the Free Plan (no credit card required). Try all features unlimited until you exceed the monthly limits.
